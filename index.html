Welcome!

My name is Davide Coluccia and I am a PhD student in Economics at Bocconi University. Originally from Rome, before coming to Milan I studied Economics at Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna and University of Pisa.

In winter 2022 I will be visiting the Center for Economic History at Northwestern University under the supervision of prof. Joel Mokyr.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Political and Labor Economics and Economic History. In my research, I exploit quasi-natural historical experiments to answer questions of contemporary interest.
