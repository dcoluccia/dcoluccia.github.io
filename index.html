About Me

My name is Davide Coluccia. I am a Postdoctoral researcher at Northwestern University's Center for Economic History. In June 2023, I obtained my Ph.D. in Economics at Bocconi University.

I am on the job market in 2023-2024.

My institutional website is available here.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Labor Economics and Economics of Innovation. In many of my projects, I take advantage of historical settings to explore economic questions which, despite being currently relevant, would be hard to study using modern data.
